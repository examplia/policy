01. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Buck ("we," "us," or "the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our Discord bot services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

02. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Discord User ID: Unique identifier assigned by Discord
  • Username and Display Name: Your current Discord username and server-specific display name
  • Server Membership: Information about which monitored servers you join or leave
  • Timestamps: When you join, leave, or interact with monitored servers
  • Public Profile Information: Avatar, account creation date, and other publicly available Discord data

2.2 Admin-Generated Information

  • Flag Reports: Records when administrators flag users as suspicious
  • Blacklist Entries: Records of users restricted from bot services
  • Moderation Notes: Additional context provided by server administrators
  • Risk Assessments: Algorithmic evaluations based on server membership patterns

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Private messages or direct message content
  • Voice chat recordings or transcripts
  • Personal information not publicly available on Discord
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location data beyond server membership

03. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • Security Monitoring: Identify potential security risks across monitored servers
  • Cross-Server Analysis: Track user activity patterns between different Discord communities
  • Risk Assessment: Generate security reports for server administrators
  • Moderation Support: Provide tools for server administrators to make informed decisions

3.2 Administrative Functions

  • Maintain historical records of user activity
  • Generate statistics and analytics for server security
  • Enable flagging and blacklisting systems
  • Provide crosscheck functionality between servers

04. INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

4.1 Authorized Sharing

  • Server Administrators: Information is shared with administrators of monitored servers
  • Cross-Server Reports: User activity may be reported across different monitored communities
  • Security Alerts: High-risk user notifications sent to relevant server staff

4.2 We Do NOT Share Information With

  • Third-party companies for marketing purposes
  • Data brokers or advertising networks
  • Unauthorized individuals or organizations
  • Government agencies (except when legally required)

4.3 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety
  • Requested by Discord for Terms of Service violations
  • Required to prevent fraud or security threats

05. DATA RETENTION

5.1 Retention Periods

  • User Activity Data: Retained indefinitely for security purposes
  • Flag Reports: Maintained until manually removed by administrators
  • Blacklist Entries: Persist until explicitly removed
  • Server Membership History: Kept for historical analysis and risk assessment

5.2 Data Deletion

  • Users may request data deletion by contacting server administrators
  • Deleted Discord accounts are automatically purged from active monitoring
  • Historical records may be retained for security and audit purposes

06. DATA SECURITY

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encrypted Storage: Database files are stored with appropriate security measures
  • Access Controls: Limited access to authorized administrators only
  • Audit Logs: All administrative actions are logged and monitored
  • Regular Updates: Bot software is regularly updated for security patches

6.2 Security Limitations

  • No system is 100% secure; we implement reasonable safeguards
  • Users are responsible for their own Discord account security
  • Report security vulnerabilities responsibly to server administrators

07. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

7.1 Access Rights

  • Request information about data collected on your account
  • Review flag reports or blacklist entries affecting you
  • Understand how your data is being used

7.2 Correction Rights

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Provide context for flagged activities
  • Appeal automated risk assessments

7.3 Deletion Rights

  • Request deletion of your data (subject to security requirements)
  • Remove yourself from monitored servers
  • Contest blacklist or flag decisions

7.4 Portability Rights

  • Request a copy of your collected data
  • Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format

08. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

  • Our services are not intended for users under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it
  • Parents may contact us to request deletion of their child's information

09. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

  • Data may be processed in different countries where Discord operates
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers
  • Data protection standards are maintained regardless of processing location

10. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

10.1 Discord Integration

  • Our bot operates through Discord's API and platform
  • Discord's Privacy Policy also applies to your interactions
  • We comply with Discord's data usage requirements

10.2 No Other Third Parties

  • We do not integrate with other social media platforms
  • No data is shared with advertising networks
  • No third-party analytics services are used

11. COOKIES AND TRACKING

  • Our bot does not use cookies or web tracking technologies
  • No behavioral advertising or profiling is conducted
  • Data collection is limited to Discord API interactions

12. PRIVACY POLICY UPDATES

12.1 Notification of Changes

  • Significant changes will be announced in monitored servers
  • Updated policies will be posted with new effective dates
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms

12.2 Review Recommendations

  • Review this policy periodically for updates
  • Contact administrators with questions about changes
  • Exercise your rights if you disagree with updates

13. CONTACT INFORMATION

13.1 Privacy Inquiries

  • Contact server administrators in monitored Discord servers
  • Submit privacy requests through appropriate Discord channels
  • Report privacy concerns to server owners or moderators

13.2 Data Protection Officer

  • For formal privacy complaints, contact the senior administrator
  • Escalate unresolved issues to Discord's Trust & Safety team
  • Seek legal advice for complex privacy matters

14. COMPLIANCE

14.1 Regulatory Compliance

  • We strive to comply with applicable privacy laws
  • GDPR rights are respected for EU users
  • CCPA rights are honored for California residents
  • Other regional privacy laws are considered

14.2 Discord Policy Compliance

  • Full compliance with Discord's Terms of Service
  • Adherence to Discord Developer Policy requirements
  • Respect for Discord's API rate limits and usage guidelines

15. DATA PROCESSING LEGAL BASIS

15.1 Legitimate Interests

  • Server security and safety protection
  • Fraud prevention and risk assessment
  • Community moderation and management

15.2 Consent

  • Continued use of Discord servers with our bot present
  • Participation in communities that utilize our services
  • Acceptance of server rules that include bot monitoring

By using Discord servers where Buck is present, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy is designed to be transparent about our data practices while protecting both user privacy and community security.
